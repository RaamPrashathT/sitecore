# Product Overview

This is a construction project management platform designed for managing construction projects, requisitions, catalogues, and site operations.

## Core Capabilities

- **Project Management**: Track projects, phases, budgets, and timelines
- **Requisition System**: Create and approve material/resource requisitions with multi-level approval workflows
- **Catalogue Management**: Maintain catalogues of materials, labour, equipment, and other resources with supplier quotes
- **Site Logging**: Document daily site activities with images and comments
- **Inventory Tracking**: Monitor inventory across multiple locations
- **Organization & Team Management**: Multi-tenant system with role-based access (Admin, Engineer, Client)
- **Supplier Management**: Track suppliers and their quotes for catalogue items
- **Notifications**: Real-time notifications for requisitions, approvals, and project updates

## User Roles

- **IDLE**: Unassigned users
- **ADMIN**: Full administrative access
- **ENGINEER**: Project execution and requisition management
- **CLIENT**: View-only access to project information

## Key Workflows

1. Create projects with phases and budgets
2. Submit requisitions for materials/resources
3. Approve/reject requisitions with budget tracking
4. Order items from suppliers
5. Log daily site activities with photos
6. Track inventory and costs
